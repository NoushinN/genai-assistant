# Appendix A: Pre‑Submission Review Checklist for AI‑Assisted Analysis {-}

This checklist supports responsible use of **generative AI assistants** in policy and health analytics. It is intended for use **before circulation, briefing, or publication** of any AI‑assisted analytical product.

---

## 1. Scope and use of AI assistance

- [ ] I can clearly describe where AI assistance was used.
- [ ] AI assistance supported thinking and expression, not decisions.
- [ ] No AI output is presented as evidence.

## 2. Factual accuracy and evidence

- [ ] All factual claims are verified against primary sources.
- [ ] No fabricated statistics, examples, or citations remain.
- [ ] Jurisdiction, population, and timeframe are correct.

## 3. Reasoning and analytical integrity

- [ ] Conclusions follow from evidence.
- [ ] Key assumptions are explicit.
- [ ] Alternatives and uncertainties are acknowledged.

## 4. Authorship and accountability

- [ ] A clear human analytical voice is present.
- [ ] Responsibility for conclusions is explicit.

## Final check

- [ ] The analysis stands without AI assistance.
